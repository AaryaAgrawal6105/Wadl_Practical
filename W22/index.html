About Us

About PICT

PICT is an autonomous engineering college affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in computer engineering, IT, and electronics.

The college is known for its strong placements, active technical communities, and vibrant student culture.

Courses Offered

B.E. Computer Engineering

4-year undergraduate program covering programming, algorithms, systems, and AI.

B.E. Information Technology

4-year program focused on web, networks, databases, and software engineering.

B.E. Electronics & Telecommunication

4-year program covering signal processing, embedded systems, and communication.

M.E. / M.Tech
